Reputation

Rankings

Guilford is ranked #173 in National Liberal Arts Colleges by U.S. News and World Report. It is ranked #434 Overall by Forbes.

Reputation

Guilford is known as one of the "Colleges that Change Lives." Although according to studentsreview.com, only 49% of those surveyed said they would return to the school, many people view Guilford as a fine liberal arts college.

News

Student Publications

The American Scholastic Press Association named The Guilfordian the “Most Outstanding College Newspaper of 2013 – 2014” in its annual best college media contest.

Academics

Academics are the highlight of Guilford, and most students find their academic experiences to be top notch. There are study abroad programs, and more than half of the classes have fewer than 20 students.

Selectivity

Guilford is not a very selective college. Its admission rate is high at 80%, and its yield is very low, at 16%.

Social Life

Guilford is a small, tight-knit community, and so everyone is friendly with each other. According to College Prowler, "Overall, students are outgoing, interested in meeting new people, and welcoming to everyone they encounter." There are parties on weekends, however Guilford doesn't have a reputation as being a party school.
